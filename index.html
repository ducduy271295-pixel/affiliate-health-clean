1

Upload the files

2

3

Add more offers later

FAQ

Quick questions

Can I change the offer names and links?

Yes. Open offers.js and edit the title, description, URL, and image path for each offer.

Can I add more than 3 offers?

Yes. The homepage cards are dynamic, so you can add as many offers as you want in the offers array.

Can I replace the images with my own?

Yes. Put your image in the images folder and update the matching image path in offers.js.
