Heart Wellness Habits After 40

After 40, many adults begin paying more attention to daily wellness routines.

Heart wellness is best approached as a routine, not a one-time fix.

Simple ideas to consider

  • Take a short walk most days of the week.
  • Build meals around colorful whole foods.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Prioritize sleep and stress management.
  • Review supplement information carefully before choosing.
