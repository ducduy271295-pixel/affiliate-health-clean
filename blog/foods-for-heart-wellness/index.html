Foods for Heart Wellness: Balanced Eating Ideas

Food choices are an important part of an overall wellness routine.

The goal is consistency, balance, and meals you can maintain.

Simple ideas to consider

  • Add fruits and vegetables to daily meals.
  • Choose fiber-rich foods when possible.
  • Include healthy fats as part of a balanced diet.
  • Reduce overly processed choices when you can.
  • Pair nutrition with movement, sleep, and hydration.
